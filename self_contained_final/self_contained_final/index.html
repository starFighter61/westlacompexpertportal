About Daniel

Daniel Zivetz is the lead computer technician and website software specialist at West LA Computer Expert. His passion for technology was ignited at an early age when a personal Dell desktop sparked his curiosity to understand and fix computers.

Over the last two decades, Daniel has dedicated himself to continuous learning and professional excellence, making him a trusted expert in PC and network support, website development, and social media marketing.

Known for his honesty, resourcefulness, and exceptional client service, Daniel's journey reflects a commitment to education and a strong work ethic. From earning CompTIA A+ and Microsoft certifications to further expanding his skills at Santa Monica College—including courses in computer programming, web design, and AWS—Daniel's professional path is defined by passion, adaptability, and strategic innovation.
